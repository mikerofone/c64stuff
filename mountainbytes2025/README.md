# TurboWobbel2k

My first ever project in C64 assembly language, which I wrote entirely during the MountainBytes.ch 2025 demoscene party.
Please also see the .nfo file.

I had no prior experience with Assembler for C64 and/or the 6502 platform, and only some very little prior exposure to
assembly for the Hitachi HD6301 processor. I learned the ropes at the party by heavily leaning on c64-wiki.com, the
KickAss documentation at theweb.dk and https://www.masswerk.at/6502/6502_instruction_set.html, as well as on the great
tutors at the Demolab (beginner's workshop).

As a result, the code is quite messy, overly commented in some regions and poorly so in others, contains dead functions
and shows many other signs of someone learning on the job under time pressure and without consequences. :) In other
words, you can tell how much fun I had!

This demo has been published (among others) at https://www.pouet.net/prod.php?which=103651 and
https://demozoo.org/productions/367504/.
